## Structured Vs Unstructured Data

Which type of data is inherently better — structured or unstructured?

---

Neither is inherently better. The choice depends on the need:
- **Structured data** (e.g., a goals table) is easier to filter and analyze directly.
- **Unstructured data** (e.g., a match highlight video) is harder to analyze but can offer richer qualitative insights when properly processed.
