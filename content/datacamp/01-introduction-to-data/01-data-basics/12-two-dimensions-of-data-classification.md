## Two Dimensions Of Data Classification

Data can be classified along two independent dimensions. Fill in the blanks:

By **presentation format**: ___ vs. ___
By **information type**: ___ vs. ___

---

By **presentation format**: **Structured** vs. **Unstructured**
By **information type**: **Quantitative** vs. **Qualitative**

These two dimensions are independent — for example, a structured table can hold either quantitative or qualitative values.
