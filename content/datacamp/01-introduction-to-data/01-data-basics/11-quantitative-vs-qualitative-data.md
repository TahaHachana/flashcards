## Quantitative Vs Qualitative Data

What is the core difference between quantitative and qualitative data?

---

The difference lies in the **type of information** they provide:
- **Quantitative data** is numerical — ideal for calculations and visualizations (e.g., temperature, scores).
- **Qualitative data** is categorical — useful for spotting patterns and understanding meaning (e.g., favorite sports, customer opinions).

Note: this is distinct from the structured/unstructured distinction, which is about *how* data is presented, not *what kind* of information it carries.
