## Data Quality

What is data quality and why is it especially critical during the data preparation step?

---

Data quality is the degree to which data is **accurate, complete, valid, and consistent** for its intended purpose.

During preparation, low-quality data must be cleaned and transformed before analysis can begin. A dataset that is inaccurate, incomplete, or inconsistently formatted will produce unreliable insights — and poor insights lead to poor decisions, regardless of how sophisticated the analysis tools are. This is why preparation can consume up to 80% of total project time.
