## Step 2 — Collecting Data

What two considerations make the data collection step more effective?

---

1. **Deliberate sourcing** — data often lives in multiple locations and forms, so being intentional about where you source it ensures you get the right data for your question.
2. **Thinking ahead to analysis** — for example, choosing between raw data and data summarized by month before collection can reduce the amount of cleaning and preparation needed in the next step.
