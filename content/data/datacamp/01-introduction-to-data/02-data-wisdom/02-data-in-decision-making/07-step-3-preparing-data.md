## Step 3 — Preparing Data

What does data preparation involve, and how time-consuming can it be?

---

Data preparation involves two main activities:
1. **Cleaning** — converting messy or low-quality data into higher-quality data through skillful manipulation.
2. **Formatting** — arranging the data into the specific structure required by the chosen analysis method.

Data preparation can be the most time-consuming phase — in some cases taking up to **80% of the total time** of the entire decision making process.
