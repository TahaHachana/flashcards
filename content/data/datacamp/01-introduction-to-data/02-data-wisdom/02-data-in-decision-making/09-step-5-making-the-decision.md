## Step 5 — Making The Decision

What two inputs should be blended when making a data-driven decision, and why?

---

A data-driven decision should blend:
1. **Analysis outcomes** — the objective insights extracted from the data.
2. **Personal experience and subject matter knowledge** — contextual understanding that the data alone cannot capture.

Combining both produces a decision far more powerful than either gut feeling or data alone. Neither input should be ignored — the analysis grounds the decision in evidence, while experience provides judgment and nuance.
