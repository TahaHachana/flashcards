## Why Gut Feeling Alone Is Insufficient

Why is relying solely on gut feeling for decisions problematic, according to the data-driven framework?

---

Gut feeling is subjective and unverifiable — it cannot be tested, repeated, or improved systematically. Data-driven decision making is a **repeatable process**: each cycle produces observations that refine future decisions. Gut feeling offers no such feedback loop, making it increasingly unreliable as decisions grow in complexity or scale.
