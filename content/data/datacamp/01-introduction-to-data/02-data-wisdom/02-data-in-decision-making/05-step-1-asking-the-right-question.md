## Step 1 — Asking The Right Question

Why is asking the right question considered the hardest step in the data-driven decision making process?

---

A clearly defined question determines everything that follows. A vague or poorly scoped question leads to collecting the wrong data, analyzing the wrong things, and ultimately making poor decisions. Taking extra time to precisely define the question prevents **scope creep** — drifting into unrelated areas — and sets the entire process up for success.
