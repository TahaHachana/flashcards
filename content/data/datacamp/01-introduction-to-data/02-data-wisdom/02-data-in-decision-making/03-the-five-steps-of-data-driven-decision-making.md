## The Five Steps Of Data-Driven Decision Making

What are the five steps of the data-driven decision making process, in order?

---

1. **Ask** — identify and clearly define the question you want to answer.
2. **Collect** — gather the right data to answer that question.
3. **Prepare** — clean and format the data for analysis.
4. **Analyze** — apply tools and techniques to extract insights.
5. **Decide** — interpret results and make an informed decision.
