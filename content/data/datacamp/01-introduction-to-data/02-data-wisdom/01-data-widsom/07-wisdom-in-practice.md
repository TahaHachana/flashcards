## Wisdom In Practice

In the DIKW example about James, what does achieving wisdom look like?

---

After moving through each pyramid level:
- **Data**: raw points such as a number, a name, a location.
- **Information**: James is 2 years old and having a party at the park tonight.
- **Knowledge**: It will likely rain tonight, making the park muddy and unsuitable for outdoor play.
- **Wisdom**: James' parents should plan **alternative indoor games** for their fifteen guests.

Wisdom is the actionable decision that emerges from tracing data all the way up through context and understanding.
