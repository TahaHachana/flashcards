## Aggregations As Metrics And KPIs

How do aggregations relate to metrics, benchmarks, and KPIs within organizations?

---

Metrics, benchmarks, and KPIs are all aggregations at their core. They distill complex business processes into something **measurable and actionable**. For example, an average order value or a monthly churn rate is simply a structured aggregation applied to business data with a specific purpose — tracking performance against a goal.
