## Understanding Business Logic Behind Aggregations

Why is it important to understand the business logic behind a KPI or aggregation, and who tends to overlook this?

---

Every aggregation is built on assumptions — about which records are included, how values are calculated, and what the metric is designed to measure. Overlooking these assumptions can lead to misinterpreting results or drawing wrong conclusions.

Both **new and experienced data practitioners** commonly overlook this. Knowing how an aggregation is constructed — and what assumptions it makes — is surprisingly critical information for any investigation or analysis.
