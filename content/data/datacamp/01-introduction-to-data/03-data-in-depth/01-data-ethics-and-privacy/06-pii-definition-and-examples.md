## PII — Definition And Examples

What is PII and why does it require special protection?

---

PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is any information that can uniquely identify an individual. Examples include:
- Full name
- Date of birth
- ID card number
- Phone number

When individuals share PII with an organization, they have a reasonable expectation that it will **not be made publicly available**. Exposing PII can lead to identity theft, financial harm, and loss of personal privacy.
