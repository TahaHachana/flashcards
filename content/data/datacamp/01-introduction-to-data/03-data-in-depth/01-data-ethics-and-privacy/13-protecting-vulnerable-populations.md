## Protecting Vulnerable Populations

Why is protecting vulnerable populations specifically highlighted as a cornerstone of ethical data use?

---

Vulnerable populations — such as children, marginalized communities, or individuals with limited digital literacy — are less able to protect themselves from data misuse or to give fully informed consent. Ethical data use requires actively accounting for these groups in both the design and ongoing monitoring of data practices, ensuring data does not disproportionately harm those least equipped to defend themselves.
