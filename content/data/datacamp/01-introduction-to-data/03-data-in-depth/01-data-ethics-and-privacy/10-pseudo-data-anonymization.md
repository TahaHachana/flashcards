## Pseudo-Data Anonymization

What is pseudo-data anonymization, and how does it balance privacy with analytical value?

---

Pseudo-data anonymization is the process of **removing PII from a dataset** so that specific data points can no longer be traced back to individuals. This makes the data anonymous while still allowing analysts to find **patterns of interest** in the data.

It balances privacy and utility: individuals' identities are protected, but the dataset retains enough information to support meaningful analysis — for example, identifying health trends without exposing individual patient identities.
