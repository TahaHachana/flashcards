## Five Principles Of Data Ethics Cloze

Fill in the five principles of data ethics and the core requirement of each:

1. ___ — ask for ___ before collecting data.
2. ___ — be open about how data is ___, ___, and ___.
3. ___ — individuals must be in control of their ___.
4. ___ — collect data only for the ___ reasons.
5. ___ — monitor the ___ of data use to prevent ___ harm.

---

1. **Permission** — ask for **consent** before collecting data.
2. **Transparency** — be open about how data is **collected**, **stored**, and **used**.
3. **Privacy** — individuals must be in control of their **personal data (PII)**.
4. **Intention** — collect data only for the **right, beneficial** reasons.
5. **Outcome** — monitor the **real-world impact** of data use to prevent **inadvertent** harm.
