## Encryption

What is encryption and what role does it play in data privacy?

---

Encryption is the process of **translating data into another form** so that only people with a digital key can read it. It is a foundational technical control for protecting sensitive data — even if an encrypted database is accessed without authorization, the data remains unreadable to anyone without the key. However, encryption alone cannot prevent all privacy breaches, as human error remains a major risk.
