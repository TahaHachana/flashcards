## Data Breaches — Causes And Prevention

What commonly causes data breaches, and what are two ways to prevent them?

---

Data breaches are often caused by **avoidable human actions** that allow malicious individuals to retrieve PII or other sensitive data. Two prevention strategies:

1. **Limit what you share** — avoid sharing PII through phishing emails, ransomware attacks, or oversharing on social media.
2. **Individual security hygiene** — use strong, unique passwords; keep operating systems up-to-date; browse the internet and use email with caution.
