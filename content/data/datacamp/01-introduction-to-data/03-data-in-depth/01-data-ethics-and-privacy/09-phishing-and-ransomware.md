## Phishing And Ransomware

What are phishing and ransomware, and how do they relate to data privacy?

---

Both are common attack methods that attempt to trick individuals into exposing PII or granting unauthorized access:
- **Phishing** — deceptive emails or messages that impersonate trusted sources to convince users to share personal information.
- **Ransomware** — malicious software that locks users out of their data or systems and demands payment for restoration.

These are "obvious" examples of why limiting what you share — even on social media — is a critical individual responsibility in protecting data privacy.
