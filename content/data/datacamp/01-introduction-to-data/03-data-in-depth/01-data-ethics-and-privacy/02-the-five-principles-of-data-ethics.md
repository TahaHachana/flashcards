## The Five Principles Of Data Ethics

What are the five key principles of data ethics?

---

According to one widely referenced framework (Harvard Business School, 2021), the five principles are:
1. **Permission** — obtain user consent before collecting data.
2. **Transparency** — be open about how data is collected, stored, and used.
3. **Privacy** — protect individuals' personal data and give them control over it.
4. **Intention** — collect and use data only for the right, beneficial reasons.
5. **Outcome** — monitor the real-world impact of data use and prevent inadvertent harm.
