## What Is Data Ethics And Why It Matters

What is data ethics, and what two goals does it serve for organizations?

---

Data ethics is the set of moral principles guiding the responsible collection, storage, and use of data. It helps organizations:
1. **Protect customers' safety** — by catching unethical data collection, storage, or use before harm occurs.
2. **Avoid legal issues** — non-compliance with ethical standards can expose organizations to regulatory penalties and reputational damage.
