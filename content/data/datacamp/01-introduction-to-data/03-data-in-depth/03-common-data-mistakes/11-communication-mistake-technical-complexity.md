## Communication Mistake — Technical Complexity

A data analyst uses sophisticated statistical techniques to analyze payment habits but the manager cannot follow the analysis. What went wrong and how could it be avoided?

---

The analyst failed to **tailor the communication to the audience**. A technically rigorous analysis is only valuable if the recipient can understand its relevance to their business question. To avoid this:
- Translate technical findings into plain business language.
- Focus on what the result *means* for decisions, not how it was computed.
- Use visuals and summaries rather than raw statistical output.

Understanding your audience is as important as the analysis itself.
