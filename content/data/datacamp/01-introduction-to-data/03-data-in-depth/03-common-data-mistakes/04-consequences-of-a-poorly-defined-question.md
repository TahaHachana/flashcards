## Consequences Of A Poorly Defined Question

What three risks arise from failing to clearly define the problem before collecting data?

---

1. **Inappropriate data collection** — gathering data that does not actually address the intended question.
2. **Flawed analysis** — analyzing the wrong variables or using the wrong methods because the goal was unclear.
3. **Incorrect conclusions** — reaching answers that do not reflect reality because the question, data, and analysis were misaligned from the start.
