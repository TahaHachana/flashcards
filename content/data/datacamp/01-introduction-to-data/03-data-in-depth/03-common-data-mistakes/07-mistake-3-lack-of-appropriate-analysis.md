## Mistake 3 — Lack Of Appropriate Analysis

Even with the right question and the right data, what can go wrong in the analysis stage?

---

Several errors can undermine the analysis stage:
1. **Jumping to conclusions** without applying proper statistical methods.
2. **Lack of context** — failing to account for external factors that explain the data.
3. **Incorrect aggregations or calculations** — using the wrong formulas or summarizations.
4. **Confusing correlation with causation** — assuming that a statistical relationship implies a direct causal link.
