## Four Data Mistakes Cloze

Fill in the four common data mistakes and the core risk of each:

1. Not clearly ___ the ___ → risk of inappropriate ___ and incorrect ___.
2. ___ or wrong ___ → findings are not ___ of the target ___.
3. Lack of appropriate ___ → jumping to ___, ignoring ___, or confusing ___ with ___.
4. Poor ___ of results → insights go ___ or lead to ___.

---

1. Not clearly **defining** the **problem** → risk of inappropriate **data collection** and incorrect **conclusions**.
2. **Insufficient** or wrong **data** → findings are not **representative** of the target **population**.
3. Lack of appropriate **analysis** → jumping to **conclusions**, ignoring **context**, or confusing **correlation** with **causation**.
4. Poor **communication** of results → insights go **unused** or lead to **misunderstandings**.
