## Stage 3 — Clean And Process

What does the clean and process stage involve, and what kinds of issues does it address?

---

Cleaning and processing prepares raw data for analysis by:
1. **Formatting** — ensuring data conforms to a consistent structure.
2. **Handling missing values or errors** — filling gaps, correcting mistakes, or removing invalid records.
3. **Transforming data** — converting data into a more usable or analysis-ready form.

This stage eliminates inconsistencies that would otherwise compromise the quality and reliability of any subsequent analysis.
