## Data Archiving

What does data archiving involve?

---

Data archiving involves preserving data in a usable format for future reference through:
1. **Backing up data** — creating copies to protect against accidental loss.
2. **Maintaining documentation** — recording what the data contains, how it was collected, and how it was used.
3. **Applying digital preservation techniques** — ensuring the data remains accessible and readable over time as technology evolves.
