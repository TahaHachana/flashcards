## Why The Data Life Cycle Matters

Why is the data life cycle important for organizations?

---

The data life cycle is important because it helps organizations:
1. **Handle data responsibly** — ensuring data is regulated in an ethical and compliant manner.
2. **Identify improvement areas** — by following the stages, organizations can spot weaknesses in their data management practices.
3. **Improve efficiency and effectiveness** — structured data handling leads to better operational outcomes and more reliable insights.
