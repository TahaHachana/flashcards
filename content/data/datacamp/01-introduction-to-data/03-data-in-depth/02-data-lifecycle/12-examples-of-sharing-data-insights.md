## Examples Of Sharing Data Insights

Give four examples of how data insights can be shared with stakeholders.

---

1. **Dashboards** — interactive visual displays of live or updated metrics.
2. **Reports** — structured written summaries of findings.
3. **Academic or industry papers** — formal documentation for peer review or publication.
4. **Conferences or presentations** — verbal and visual communication of findings to an audience.

Making datasets available to colleagues or other researchers is also a form of sharing that enables further analysis.
