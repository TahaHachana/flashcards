## Methods And Tools For Analysis And Visualization

What types of methods and tools are used in the analyze and visualize stage?

---

The analyze and visualize stage uses a wide range of methods and tools, including:
- **Statistical methods** — for summarizing data, identifying relationships, and drawing inferences.
- **Machine learning algorithms** — for predictive modeling and pattern detection.
- **Programming languages** — such as Python and R.
- **Software tools** — such as Tableau, Power BI, Excel, and Google Sheets.

The right choice depends on the complexity of the question and the nature of the data.
