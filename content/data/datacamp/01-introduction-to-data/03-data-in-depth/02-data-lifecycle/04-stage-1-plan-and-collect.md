## Stage 1 — Plan And Collect

What four key decisions are made during the planning stage of the data life cycle?

---

During planning, organizations decide:
1. **What question** the data should answer — aligned with stakeholder needs.
2. **What type of data** is required to answer that question.
3. **How the data will be managed** throughout its life cycle.
4. **Who will be responsible** for the data at each stage.

These decisions shape every subsequent stage, making the planning stage one of the most consequential.
