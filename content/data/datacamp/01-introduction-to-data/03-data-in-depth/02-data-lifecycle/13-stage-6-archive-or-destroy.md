## Stage 6 — Archive Or Destroy

What determines whether data should be archived or destroyed at the end of the data life cycle?

---

The decision is based on the **initial plan** and the ongoing value vs. risk of retaining the data:
- **Archive** if the data may be needed for future analysis, compliance, or reference.
- **Destroy** if retaining the data poses a privacy risk (e.g., it contains PII that is no longer needed) or to free up storage resources.

The choice should be made deliberately — neither archiving nor destroying data should be the default without consideration.
