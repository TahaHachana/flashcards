## Stage 5 — Share

Why is the share stage considered vital yet often overlooked in the data life cycle?

---

An insightful analysis that is never communicated to others has **no practical value** — insights only create impact when they reach the people who can act on them. Despite this, the sharing stage is frequently underestimated. Successfully communicating results to stakeholders is as important as the analysis itself, yet data practitioners often focus almost exclusively on the technical work.
