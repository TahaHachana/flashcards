## Types Of Data Captured And Their Uses

What four types of data does shop two capture, and what business decision does each support?

---

| Data Type | Business Decision |
|---|---|
| Sales data per product/flavor | Identify top and poor sellers |
| Stock data (barcode scanning) | Avoid running out of popular flavors |
| Weather data | Predict sales spikes on hot days |
| Cost and revenue data | Calculate margins and adjust pricing |
