## Evolution Of Data Storage Timeline

List the major milestones in data storage history in chronological order.

---

1. **DNA** — nature has always stored complete genetic information this way.
2. **Cave and wall paintings** — earliest human record-keeping (thousands of years ago).
3. **Papyrus and parchment scrolls** — used by Chinese, Egyptian, and Roman civilizations to track financial systems.
4. **Punch cards** — popularized in the 1890s.
5. **Magnetic tape and floppy disks** — replaced punch cards.
6. **CDs, hard drives, and solid state drives (SSDs)** — high-capacity local digital storage.
7. **Cloud storage** — data centralized in data centers via the Internet; one of today's most popular methods.
