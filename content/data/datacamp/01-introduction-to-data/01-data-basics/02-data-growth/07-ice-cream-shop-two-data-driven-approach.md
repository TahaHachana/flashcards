## Ice Cream Shop Two — Data-Driven Approach

What practices make ice cream shop two data-driven?

---

Shop two:
- Sells 20+ flavors with a spare box of each kept in stock.
- Uses an **electronic cash register** to monitor every sale.
- **Scans barcodes** to continuously measure stock levels.
- Collects **weather data** to anticipate demand changes.
- Combines **cost and revenue data** to calculate margins per product.

This allows it to avoid stockouts, replace poor-selling flavors, predict sales spikes, and optimize pricing.
