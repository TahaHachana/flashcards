## Aggregation

What is data aggregation? Give four examples of common aggregation metrics.

---

Aggregation is the process of summarizing many individual data points into simpler metrics to make large datasets interpretable. Common aggregation metrics:
1. **Sum** — e.g., total sales revenue.
2. **Average** — e.g., mean daily temperature.
3. **Minimum / Maximum** — e.g., lowest and highest selling flavors.
4. **Mode** — e.g., most frequently purchased flavor.

In the ice cream shop example, aggregating sales per flavor is what enables decisions about which products to keep or replace.
