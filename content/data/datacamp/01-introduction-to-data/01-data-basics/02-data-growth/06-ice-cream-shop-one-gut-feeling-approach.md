## Ice Cream Shop One — Gut Feeling Approach

How does ice cream shop one make decisions, and what is the consequence?

---

Shop one sells only three fixed flavors (vanilla, chocolate, strawberry). When a flavor runs out, it simply refills it the next day without questioning which flavors sell best or why. This gut-feeling approach leads to suboptimal decisions — such as keeping poor-selling flavors — and potentially losing sales.
