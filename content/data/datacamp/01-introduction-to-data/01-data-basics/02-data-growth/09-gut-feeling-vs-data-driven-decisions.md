## Gut Feeling Vs Data-Driven Decisions

Why do data-driven decisions outperform gut-feeling decisions in the long run?

---

Gut feeling often leads to suboptimal choices — for example, replacing the wrong ice cream flavors and losing sales as a result. Data-driven decisions are grounded in evidence, making them more reliable and repeatable. In the long term, optimizing decisions through data results in increased revenue and operational efficiency.
