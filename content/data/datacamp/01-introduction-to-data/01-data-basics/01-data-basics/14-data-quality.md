## Data Quality

What is data quality and what four properties define it?

---

Data quality is the degree to which data is fit for its intended purpose. It is assessed across four properties:
1. **Accurate** — correctly reflects reality.
2. **Complete** — no important values are missing.
3. **Valid** — conforms to expected formats and rules.
4. **Consistent** — does not contradict other data in the dataset.
