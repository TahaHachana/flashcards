## Audience Analysis — Full Framework

Summarize the complete audience analysis framework introduced in this section. What three questions do you ask, and what does each one inform?

---

| Question | What It Informs |
|----------|----------------|
| What **role** does the audience perform? | Which aspects of the insight are most relevant; the angle and priorities to address |
| What do they **know vs. need to know**? | The appropriate depth, technical level, and what assumptions to address |
| How well do they **know you**? | The level of formality, amount of context needed, and number of iteration cycles to plan for |

Together, these three questions form the foundation of a tailored **communication playbook** for each stakeholder.
