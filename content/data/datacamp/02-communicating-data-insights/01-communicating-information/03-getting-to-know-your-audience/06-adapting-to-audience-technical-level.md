## Adapting to Audience Technical Level

Using the Buildingsway case, contrast how Harold should adapt his communication for Paul versus Yuri and Lorraine.

---

| Stakeholder | Technical Level | Appropriate Approach |
|-------------|----------------|----------------------|
| **Paul** (Head of Analytics) | High | Go deep into processes and analytics; justify methodology |
| **Yuri** (Warehouse Manager) | Low | Keep to results; focus on what items to stock and their availability |
| **Lorraine** (Head of Sales) | Low | Keep to results; focus on anticipated popularity of new options |

The key principle: **match the depth of the message to the audience's technical background**.
