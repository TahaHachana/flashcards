## Role of Stakeholder Roles in Communication Planning

Why does knowing a stakeholder's role within the organization matter when planning how to communicate data insights?

---

A stakeholder's role determines:
- Their **priorities and concerns** (e.g., a warehouse manager cares about stock; a sales head cares about popularity)
- The **angle** from which they will evaluate your insights
- What aspects of the data are most **relevant** to include or emphasize

Tailoring the message to each role increases the chance of buy-in and action.
