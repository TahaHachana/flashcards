## Buildingsway Case — Cloze Summary

Complete the following summary of the Buildingsway case:

Harold is an ________ at Buildingsway. His research led to two key insights: focus on ________ treehouses and add ________ to standard designs. To communicate these, he tailored his approach to three stakeholders. Paul needed ________ detail because he has a strong technical background. Yuri needed to know about ________, and Lorraine needed to know about ________. With Paul, Harold planned to be more ________ since it was their first interaction.

---

Harold is an **analyst** at Buildingsway. His research led to two key insights: focus on **indoor** treehouses and add **retractable roofs** to standard designs. To communicate these, he tailored his approach to three stakeholders. Paul needed **deep analytical/process** detail because he has a strong technical background. Yuri needed to know about **stock items and their availability**, and Lorraine needed to know about **anticipated popularity of the new options**. With Paul, Harold planned to be more **formal** since it was their first interaction.
