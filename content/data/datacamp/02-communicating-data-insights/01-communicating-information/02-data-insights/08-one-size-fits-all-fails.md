## Why One-Size-Fits-All Fails for Insights

Why does a one-size-fits-all approach to communicating insights fail, and what principle should replace it?

---

A one-size-fits-all approach fails because **the same insight looks completely different for different stakeholders** depending on how it affects their specific roles and responsibilities.

The replacing principle: tailor the insight and its supporting context **to each audience** — an approach that tries to suit everyone ultimately suits no one.
