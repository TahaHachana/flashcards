## Examples of Operational Insights

Give four examples of high-level operational insights that drive business decisions.

---

1. Determining the right **product mix** for next year's sales forecast
2. **Minimizing risk** while managing expenses
3. **Optimizing employee overtime** scheduling
4. Managing **supply chain disruptions**

Note: Insights can also impact smaller outcomes and still be valuable — they are not limited to large-scale operations.
