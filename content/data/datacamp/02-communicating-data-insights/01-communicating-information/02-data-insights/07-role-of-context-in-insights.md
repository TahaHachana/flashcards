## The Role of Context in Insights

Why is context vital when sharing insights, and what is typically needed beyond business objectives alone?

---

Context is vital because it helps the audience **understand the scope and significance** of the findings — without it, insights can be misread or undervalued.

Business objectives are only the starting point. **Additional background information** is often required to give the audience enough supporting information to fully understand what the insight means and how it applies to their specific role.
