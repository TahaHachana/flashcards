## Insight Process Cloze

Fill in the blanks to complete the insight discovery process:

Collect data → ________ the data → ________ it through analytical techniques → ________ the knowledge to business actions → ________ the insight and action plan → observe the outcome.

---

Collect data → **Organize** the data → **Distill** it through analytical techniques → **Apply** the knowledge to business actions → **Communicate** the insight and action plan → observe the outcome.
