## Best Practices for Communicating Insights — Full Summary

What are the four best practices for building a strong foundation when communicating insights?

---

1. **Keep key business objectives in mind** — use shared organizational language to ground and contextualize insights
2. **Provide sufficient context** — go beyond objectives; add background so the audience understands the scope of findings
3. **Tailor to each stakeholder** — the same insight means different things to different roles; avoid one-size-fits-all
4. **Keep it simple** — actionable insights are more valuable than complex ones that cannot be implemented
