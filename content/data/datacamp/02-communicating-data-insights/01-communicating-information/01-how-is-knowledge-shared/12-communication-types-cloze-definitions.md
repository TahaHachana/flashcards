## Communication Types Cloze — Definitions

Complete the following:

- ________ communication uses text on a page or in a presentation.
- ________ communication uses charts, graphs, pictures, and videos.
- ________ communication uses facial expressions, posture, and eye contact.
- ________ communication is anything spoken and heard.

---

- **Written** communication uses text on a page or in a presentation.
- **Visual** communication uses charts, graphs, pictures, and videos.
- **Nonverbal** communication uses facial expressions, posture, and eye contact.
- **Verbal** communication is anything spoken and heard.
